About This App

Global Weather Forecast provides accurate, real-time weather data for any location worldwide. Our app combines multiple data sources to give you the most comprehensive weather information available.

How It Works

Simply enter a city name or use your current location to get instant weather updates. The app displays current conditions, detailed forecasts, and important environmental data like air quality.

Data Privacy

We respect your privacy. Location data is only used to fetch weather information and is not stored on our servers. All data is securely transmitted using encryption.
